How to Stay Healthy as a Student

  • Balanced Diet: Eat regular meals, include all food groups, stay hydrated, and choose healthy snacks.
  • Regular Exercise: Aim for at least 30 minutes of activity daily, including simple exercises and stretching.
  • Sleep Hygiene: Get 7-9 hours of sleep, follow a consistent sleep schedule, and avoid late-night cramming.
  • Mental Health Care: Manage stress with mindfulness, stay connected with others, and seek help when needed.
  • Time Management: Organize tasks, use study techniques like Pomodoro, and avoid procrastination.
  • Healthy Habits: Limit screen time, practice good hygiene, and avoid harmful substances.
  • Health Checkups: Keep up with routine medical exams and be aware of available health services.
